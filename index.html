Bubble Sort

Bubble sort is a sorting algorithm that repeatdly steps through a list, compares adjacent elements, and swaps them if they aren't in order. The bubble name comes from the way smaller or larger elements "bubble" to the top of the array. In the following GIFs you can see dancers at Sapientia University in Romania expressing several sorting algorithms through the use of Hungarian folk dances. It has a quadratic time complexity of O(N^2), and a constant space complexity of O(1).
